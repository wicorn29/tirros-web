About Us

Tirros is a next-generation DNS provider focused on speed, security, and user privacy. Whether you're running a personal server or an enterprise network, Tirros keeps your lookups fast and your data private.

Features

Fully COPPA Compliant

Tirros adheres to the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and ensures that no personal information is collected from children under the age of 13 without explicit parental consent.

Protection Against Phishing and Malware

Our DNS blocks websites that impersonate trusted services or distribute harmful software, ensuring that your device is safe from infections and malicious attacks.

Blocking of Ads and Trackers

Tirros protects your privacy by blocking invasive ads and behavioral trackers at the DNS level, reducing the risk of fingerprinting and unwanted data collection.

No Stalkerware or Spyware

We prioritize user privacy by blocking domains known to distribute stalkerware or spyware that covertly monitor your activities without consent.

Sexually Explicit Content Blocked

Tirros blocks access to pornographic or graphically sexual content, ensuring a safe online environment for families and users of all ages.

No Online Dating Services

To maintain a family-friendly environment, Tirros blocks domains related to dating, hookups, and adult companionship services.
