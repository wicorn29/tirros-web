Why does Tirros block content?

If you came across a site or service that has been blocked by Tirros, there is a reason for it. This is part of our mission to provide a safe, family-friendly, and privacy-respecting internet experience.

Common Block Reasons:

  • Not COPPA Compliant: A site may collect personal information from children under 13 without following the rules of the Childrenâ€™s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
  • Phishing or Malware: A site may impersonate trusted services or distribute harmful software that can infect your device.
  • Ad or Tracking Servers: A site may include invasive ads, behavioral trackers, or attempt to fingerprint your device.
  • Stalkerware or Spyware: Some services or apps covertly monitor activity and violate user privacy or safety guidelines.
  • Sexually Explicit Content: Pornographic or graphically sexual material is restricted to ensure general-audience safety.
  • Online Dating Services: Domains focused on dating, hookups, or adult companionship are blocked to maintain a family-safe environment.
  • Uncategorized or Suspicious Content: Newly registered, unverified, or suspicious domains may be temporarily restricted pending review.

If you believe a site was incorrectly blocked or wish to request a review, please tap or click the blocked page several times.
