How to Set Up Tirros

To use Tirros DNS, follow these steps:

  1. Option 1: Set DNS (Recommended):
    • Go to your router or device's DNS settings. (If you're doing this on your rouer, itt's different for every router. Look up how to change DNS on your router)
    • Set the primary DNS to the DNS listed above.
    • Please note that with this method, when you leave your home wifi the network will become unfiltered.
  2. Option 2: Download Configuration Profile (Apple Devices):
    • Open this page on an apple device, and then click the Download Profile button.
    • The device will say the page is trying to download a configuration profile, click allow.
    • Open settings, and at the top there will be a banner saying "Profile Downloaded"
    • Click on it and follow the on-screen instructions.
    • Please note that with this method, Tirros will defend the device even away from your home network.
